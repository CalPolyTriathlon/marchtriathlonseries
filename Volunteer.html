Volunteers

MTS isn't possible without the help of amazing volunteers. If you are interested in volunteering, please fill out the form below.
Volunteers will receive a free volunteer T-Shirt and food on race morning for helping out.


Click here to sign up to volunteer!

There are a ton of supportive and fun positions available throughout the event. After signing up, our volunteer coordinator will contact you a week or two prior to the event (via email or phone) to discuss what positions and times work best for your schedule.


Here is a list of some of the many positions available throughout the event:

  • Registration
  • Aid Stations
  • Transition Area
  • Run Course
  • Bike Course
  • Check in
  • Finish Line

Nonprofit? We offer donation incentives for nonprofits that make a big showing! Make this a party and let's have this event benefit our local community! Email us at caitken@calpoly.edu to find out more!
